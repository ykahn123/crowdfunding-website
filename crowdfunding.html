What is crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding is a method of raising money from a large number of people, typically via the internet. It allows individuals or organizations to gather small contributions from many supporters to fund a project, business, or cause. There are various platforms, such as Kickstarter, Indiegogo, and Go FundMe that facilitate this process.
It has become a popular way for entrepreneurs, artists, and non-profits to gain financial support while engaging with their communities.
